Always wanted to go for a hike?

But wasn't sure how to plan or what to take?

Planning a hike is really hard. All of the information is scattered around different sites and it can be extremely time consuming to look through everything. Additionally, searching for hikes with specific requirements like a certain season or difficulty can make the process even more tedious.

Trekr

Every trek begins with Trekr

Trekr aims to solve these problems with a searchable list of nearby hikes with detailed information for each. You can filter the list based on given parameters, and learn more about the hike they are interested in from the hike profiles.

  • Search and Filter

    Find the hike that best suites you

    First time you open the app, it presents a list of nearby hikes sorted based on the distance from your location. You can filter these based on your preferred length, and difficulty or directly search for it by name.

  • Hike Profile

    All the information you will need to plan your next hike

    Once you find a hike you are interested in, you can tap on it to learn more about it in the hike's profile. The hike profile contains tailored information, authored by other professional hikers, on what you might need to know about the hike before heading there. It contains information about the equipment you would need to carry, amenities available, and any special attractions.
