Presentation logistics are tough

No consistent hardware and software standard

Display ports, converters, file formats, software compatibility, there are too many variations, and no default standard. SlideCinch attempts to solve it by providing a smartphone based streaming solution.

SlideCinch

Makes slideshows a cinch

SlideCinch streams your presentations to the conference desktop/laptop from your smartphone over WiFi, and lets you control it with the app.

  • Branding

    Friendly and distinct

    As mentioned by Google's design guidelines, "...[Product icons] communicate the core idea and intent of a product." The app icon/logo here shows phone beaming signal towards a projector screen.

  • Launch website

    Measuring interest for product

    To gauge the need and interest for the product we conducted an initial website launch with analytics. We also allowed sign-ups for updates on development and launch and got over 7000 unique visitors out of which 240 signed up for an early access.

  • Present and control

    All essential information and controls on phone

    View the slides with speaker notes directly on your phone for quick reference while presenting. Check your progress, and control the slides through volume buttons.
