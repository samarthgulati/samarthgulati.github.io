Emerging market of Travel enthusiasts and Visual Storytellers

But can't fly and capture at same time

There is a growing number of travellogers and enthusiasts who are taking drones along with them when they go for their next trip.

But from our interviews with drone owners we found that they found the current mobile based UIs complicated to use. The interfaces are not built for them to shoot videos and fly at same time.

Drone You Eye

Intuitive, easy to use interface

Drone You Eye provides an easy to understand navigation interface for beginners. The screen provides all the required information to fly the drone with the footage streamed directly in the background.

  • Trace-back

    Record trajectory and trace it back

    The app allows the user to fly the drone and record its trajectory. Then they can modify the recorded path and once they are satisfied with it, with a press of a button they can ask the drone to re-trace it.

  • Pre-recorded paths

    Library of commonly taken shots

    Lets the users take shots like 360-panorama, panning, and hyperlapse, or save their own commonly taken shots to the library and share with others.
